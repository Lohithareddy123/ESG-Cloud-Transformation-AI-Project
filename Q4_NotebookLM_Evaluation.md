# Q4 - NotebookLM Evaluation

## Purpose
NotebookLM was evaluated as an AI-assisted academic and ESG document analysis tool.

## Features Tested
- Document summarisation.
- Question answering.
- Source-based responses.
- Study guide generation.

## Results
NotebookLM successfully summarised ESG documents and provided context-aware answers while maintaining source traceability.

## Limitations
- Dependent on quality of uploaded documents.
- Potential for over-reliance by users.
- AI outputs should be validated.
